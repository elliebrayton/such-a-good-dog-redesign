About Us

We emphasize positive reinforcement and clear communication. We specialize in helping you better communicate with your dog through understanding their body language and quirks.

We offer smaller size classes (four dogs per class) so each dog still gets individualized attention. We also help address behavior issues in our private sessions, and work on basic obedience. We hope that our classes can help foster a closer bond between you and your pup.

Our Trainers

Laurel Rose

Laurel has been working with dogs and their owners professionally since 2001. She is also a Certified Mentor Trainer to Animal Behavior College Students and an AKC Canine Good Citizen Instructor/Evaluator. Her interest in training began as a puppy raiser and volunteer coordinator for Canine Companions for Independence. She’s a published author of training articles on examiner.com and has been the “Ask the Expert” on WAAY TV. Laurel is also the Founder and President of Huntsville Got Your Six a registered 501(c)3 organization providing no cost PTSD service dog training for veterans and their dogs.​

Certifications: CPDT-KA

Jennie Hudson

She has been a professional trainer since 2016 and is a professionally certified trainer through the Certification Council of Professional Dog Trainers. Jennie has been heavily involved in Therapy Dog work within a local organization, and with her own dog has been a Therapy Dog team since 2014. She’s been working with rescue dogs since 2008 and is on the Advisory Board of Huntsville Animal Services, currently as President, and on their Foundation, 501(c)3 fundraising organization. She is also on the Board for SNAP (Spay Neuter Action Project). Jennie is a Certified Pet Partners Team evaluator. ​

Certifications: CPDT-KA

Gary Hudson

He graduated with honors from the Animal Behavior College as an educationally certified dog obedience instructor and is also professionally certified through the Certification Council of Professional Dog Trainers. He has been involved with rescue for 10 years as a foster, volunteer, and evaluator. Gary lives with 3 Australian Shepherds

Certifications: ABCDT, CPDT-KA
