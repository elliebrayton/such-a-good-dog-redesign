Classes Offered

This is the current classes we offer. Our classes are small to provide you and your pup more individualized attention, with a 4 dog max limit. See schedule to view class availability and times.

For dogs 6 months or younger

Puppy Gymboree

  • Prerequisite: None
    • Price: $25
    • Duration: Single Session

For puppies 8-14 weeks old at class time. This class is all about exposing your puppy to new things like tunnels, jumps, uneven surfaces, other puppies and people, all to help them build confidence during this important stage of their life.

Kindergarten

  • Prerequisite: None
    • Price: $115
    • Duration: 5 Weeks

Sit, down, stay, loose leash walking, leave it/drop it, recall, all the basics plus we address issues like jumping, nipping, house training.

For dogs 6 months or older

Elementary

  • Prerequisite: None
    • Price: $115
    • Duration: 5 Weeks

Sit, down, stay, loose leash walking, leave it/drop it, recall, all the basics plus we address issues like jumping, nipping, house training

Jr. High

  • Prerequisite: Kindergarten or Elementary or free evaluation
    • Price: $115
    • Duration: 5 Weeks

For those of any age who already know the basics and need to add a bit more distraction

High School

  • Prerequisite: Jr. High
    • Price: $105
    • Duration: 5 Weeks

Take everything you’ve learned in previous classes and add real world distractions with games and field trips!

Field Trips

  • Prerequisite: Jr. High
    • Price: $105
    • Duration: 5 Weeks

It’s just like it sounds, 4 weeks of classes in the real world practicing everything you’ve learned!
